The Elite Equine Hunter Jumper & Equitation Forum is a place for riders, trainers, and horse enthusiasts to connect and share knowledge about the hunter jumper world. Members can discuss training techniques, horse care, show strategies, tack recommendations, and experiences from the show circuit — all within a community that exclusively understands and shares the hunter jumper world.

Why a Hunter Jumper-Specific Forum?

General equestrian forums mix every discipline — Western, dressage, racing, trail riding, and hunter jumper all sharing the same space. When a hunter jumper rider posts a question about 3'6" junior hunter horses or the ASPCA Maclay, they want answers from people who actually compete in those divisions — not general horse advice from riders who have never been to a rated hunter jumper show. The Elite Equine forum is built exclusively for the hunter jumper community — meaning every member, every response, and every discussion is grounded in the same world you live in.

Connect, Not Just Browse

The forum is seamlessly integrated with Elite Equine Marketplace's full platform — meaning a forum discussion about which type of schoolmaster horse is best for a junior equitation rider can directly link to available schoolmaster listings on the marketplace. Discussions about tack recommendations connect to tack listings. Conversations about trainers connect to trainer service listings. The forum is not isolated from the marketplace — it is woven into it, making the connection between community knowledge and marketplace action immediate and seamless.
